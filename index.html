The European cat, also known as the European Shorthair, is one of the oldest and most popular cat breeds in Europe. Its origins date back to the cats that accompanied the Romans during their conquests, playing a key role in pest control.

This breed is known for its sturdiness, independent nature, and remarkable adaptability. With a short coat, a variety of colors, and a loving yet reserved personality, the European cat makes an ideal companion for any home.
